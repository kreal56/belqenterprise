Emily L. Quiambao

OWNER of BELQ ENTERPRISE

BELQ MODULARS has group of associates, all of which are contributing unique expertise to the

architecutal and interior design practice. The principal and associates then strive to develop

a unique support with each client based on open communication and personal commitment to the projects


In response to function, our professional practice centers on the exploration of inovative and excellent service


BELQ ENTERPRISE primary programs in involving site production emphasizes on

the highest quality design, time management and customary budget


BELQ MODULARS is a company founded by Emily Quiambao, an interior design graduate and part-time

designer/ space planner. The CEO of BELQ MODULARS not only saw potential income that will fuel

and expand the company, it is also in her interest to build an environment for her as an individual

to grow and be her own boss as well as a leader to his colleagues.


With BELQ MODULARS being just a new and fresh face in the furniture industry, there is still a vast room to grow.

The company itself is currently and has always been giving quality service to its customers and is living up to

the high standards that is expected thanks to the company's professional and reliable staff.
