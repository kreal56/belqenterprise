SCHEMATIC DESIGN PHASE


Schematic design is the first phase. In this step, we here at BELQ Enterprise get the chance to have a conversasion with the client to determine the project requirements and their desired goals.


CLIENT CONSULTATION

Consulting with an interior designer is a key step in creating the modular home of your dreams. This phase of a project is approach differently by each designer. Here at BELQ Enterprise, we take on a unique approach which would allows us to provide innovation to the client's needs.


FURNITURE INVENTORY

A furniture inventory is an important part of the successful result of the planning of new or renewed premises. BELQ Enterprise maps, identifies and lists all the items of furniture in use and determine the best solution for them, which can even lengthen the furniture lifecycle.


INFORMATION GATHERING and FAMILIARIZATION

In order for us to effectively define and create a interior concept for our clients we need to immerse ourselves into the business and understand: its mission, vision, target markets, culture, competitive edge, strengths and weaknesses, marketing strategies, aim, challenges and aspirations for the future.

SCHEMATIC DESIGN PHASE


Here at BELQ Enterprise, we usually start with rough study drawings that illustrate the basic concepts of the customer's design. We would often includes spatial relationships as well as basic scale and forms that the owner might desire.


SITE MEASUREMENT and VERIFICATION


Through this stage, we would be going through the construction stages by completing field verification and taking exact measurements at the construction site, following construction activities closely with the drawings and specifications.


BUILDING DESIGN and PLAN


During this time, BELQ Enterprise will start working around with ideas like finishing details, furniture, color, equipment and related objects and spaces that their client's request


SPACE PROGRAMMING/RELATIONSHIP

Space programming is a primary task during the schematic design process, to produce a geometric configuration of a space layout that is in accordance with the project's requirements.By nature, space programming is an iterative process that evolves according to the client's requirements.
