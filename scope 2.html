DESIGN DEVELOPMENT PHASE


Design development collects the results from the schematic design phase and takes them one step further. This phase involves finalizing the design and specifying such items as materials and finishes, we will show you our extensive portfolio to visualize how we work.


INITIAL PRESENTATION AND REVISION STAGE:

We meet to see if we are still on the right track. Specifications, floor plan and perspective are presented to bring your project to life. If revisions are needed, we will continue the initial design phase.


BUDGETARY COST:

We will present a quotation that we are both in agreement as to targeted budget range.
